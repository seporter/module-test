Account Surrender Refresher

Welcome to the Account Surrender Refresher module. This module will guide you through the Account Surrender process at StubHub, explain how it works, and provide troubleshooting steps. Let's get started!

Account Surrender is a process used by StubHub on an event-by-event basis, specifically when mobile ticket transfers are restricted by event organizers. It is applicable when transfers are closed, or a single ticket cannot be transferred. Sellers MUST BE SELECTED for this fulfillment method and are CLEARLY IDENTIFIED through work items and banners.

Sellers identified for Account Surrender will have a clear work item and banner under each transaction. They must send their credentials directly to the buyer using StubHub's template. This process only works if the account contains the exact number of tickets sold to the buyer. Sellers are responsible for ensuring the correct number of tickets are available in the account.

Steps for Sellers:

  • Identify Account Surrender transactions through work items and banners.
  • Send account credentials to the buyer using the provided template.
  • Ensure the account contains the exact number of tickets sold.

The buyer cannot access the account:

  • Contact the seller to obtain the correct login details.
  • If unresolved by the deadline, the order should be subbed.

The buyer did not receive credentials from the seller:

  • Contact the seller and provide login details with a strict deadline.
  • If the seller does not meet the deadline, the order will be subbed.

The account is empty or contains fewer tickets than purchased:

  • Contact the seller to provide the correct account with the exact number of tickets.
  • If unresolved within the deadline, sub the order.
  • For brokers, escalate to Seller Support. For others, call the seller for correct information.
  • If there is no answer, leave a detailed voicemail and escalate to Tier 2 Chat for follow-up.
